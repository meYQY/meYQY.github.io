Why a Gap Year?

Sample template — replace with your own story.

The core reason: spend enough time in a real environment to train the 0→1 skillset—spot opportunities, build products, and be accountable to users and outcomes.

Three things to validate

  • Can I reliably turn an idea into something usable (MVP → MRR)?
  • Do I enjoy and withstand high-density uncertainty (cadence and review)?
  • Can I create compounding value within a team (communication, judgment, speed)?

How I structure it

  • Days: building and product engineering; nights: writing and review.
  • Audit courses to expand perspective (philosophy, AI, Chinese, math).
  • Biweekly review: data, narrative, and next steps.

What I expect

Whatever the scale of outcomes, I aim to turn passion and luck into repeatable capability and perspective. Applications should show not just scores and honors, but a verifiable trail of action.
